About Taka Studio

Based in Istanbul, Taka Studio is a small, ambitious team creating original, design-driven games with clarity and craft. The name “Taka” refers to a traditional small boat from Türkiye’s Black Sea coast — a symbol of exploration, resilience, and handmade quality.

  • • Values: Creativity, Clarity, Collaboration, Craftsmanship
  • • Focus: PC-first strategy and systems-driven experiences
  • • Engine: Unreal Engine 5
