Description

HexaFootball is a turn-based football strategy game for PC that blends chess-like tactical depth with the drama of football. Played on a hex-based pitch, the game focuses on positioning, foresight, and calculated decisions rather than reflex-based action.

History

HexaFootball began as a passion project by two friends studying game design, both deeply interested in strategy and sports games. The project started with a simple question: what kind of football game would we want to play ourselves? Development officially began in mid-2025.

Features

  • ● Turn-based tactical gameplay
  • ● Hexagonal grid positioning
  • ● Career & Managerial mode
  • ● Competitive Multiplayer
