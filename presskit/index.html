Description

Taka Studio is an independent game studio based in Istanbul crafting design‑driven experiences. We focus on PC-first strategy games and systems-driven experiences that prioritize clarity and craftsmanship.

Our goal is to create worlds that players can explore "move by move," combining tactical depth with elegant aesthetics.

History

The name “Taka” refers to a traditional small boat from Türkiye’s Black Sea coast — a symbol of exploration, resilience, and handmade quality. Founded by a small team of passionate developers, the studio aim to bring a fresh perspective to the indie strategy scene.
