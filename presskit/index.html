Description

HexaFootball is a turn-based football strategy game that reimagines the sport through tactical decision-making rather than reflex-driven action. Every pass, movement, and shot takes place on a hex-based grid where foresight and planning define success.

Inspired by board games and classic strategy titles, HexaFootball blends the emotional highs of football with the mental depth of turn-based gameplay.

Key Features

  • Turn-based football gameplay on a hexagonal grid
  • Positioning, passing lanes, and tactical foresight over reflexes
  • Clear, readable UI focused on strategic clarity
  • PC-first design with competitive and single-player modes

Development History

HexaFootball began as an experiment to answer a simple question: what if football was played like chess? Developed by Taka Studio, the project evolved through rapid prototyping into a full-scale strategy game, focusing on systemic depth, readability, and meaningful decisions every turn.
