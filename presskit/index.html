HexaFootball

Pitch

HexaFootball is a turn-based football strategy game that blends the tactical depth of chess with the emotional intensity of football. Every pass, movement, and shot is a calculated decision rather than a test of reflexes.

HexaFootball is a turn-based football strategy game for PC that blends chess-like tactical depth with the drama of football. Played on a hex-based pitch, the game focuses on positioning, foresight, and calculated decisions rather than reflex-based action.
