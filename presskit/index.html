HexaFootball

Pitch

HexaFootball is a turn-based football strategy game that blends the tactical depth of chess with the emotional intensity of football. Every pass, movement, and shot is a calculated decision rather than a test of reflexes.

HexaFootball is a turn-based football strategy game for PC that blends chess-like tactical depth with the drama of football. Played on a hex-based pitch, the game focuses on positioning, foresight, and calculated decisions rather than reflex-based action.

The game features both single-player and multiplayer modes, including a career mode where players define their managerial style, build their squad, and develop long-term strategies. From shaping your team identity to outthinking human opponents online, HexaFootball delivers a football experience driven by planning, not speed.

History

HexaFootball began as a passion project by iki game design öğrencisi arkadaşın, strateji ve spor oyunlarına duydukları derin ilgiyle başladı. Proje basit bir soruyla yola çıktı: "Kendimiz oynamak isteyeceğimiz nasıl bir futbol oyunu tasarlardık?"

Driven by a shared love for tactical depth and thoughtful decision-making, they set out to create a football experience that moves away from reflex-based gameplay and toward pure strategy. Development officially began in mid-2025, and through continuous iteration, internal playtests, and design refinements, HexaFootball evolved into a distinct, turn-based tactical football experience built for PC.

Features

  • Turn-based football gameplay where strategy, positioning, and foresight matter more than reflexes
  • Hexagonal grid-based pitch that transforms football into a chess-like tactical board
  • Deep tactical decision-making inspired by board games and classic strategy systems
  • Career mode with managerial style choices, squad building, and long-term team development
  • Multiplayer mode allowing players to outthink real opponents in competitive tactical matches
  • Team building and player management systems focused on roles, synergy, and positioning
  • Clean, readable, and information-focused UI designed for planning and analysis
  • PC-exclusive experience created for players who enjoy thoughtful, deliberate gameplay
