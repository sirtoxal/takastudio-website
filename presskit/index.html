Description

HexaFootball is a turn-based football strategy game that reimagines the sport through tactical decision-making rather than reflex-driven action. Every pass, movement, and shot takes place on a hex-based grid where foresight and planning define success.

Key Features

  • Turn-based football gameplay on a hexagonal grid
  • Positioning, passing lanes, and tactical foresight
  • Clear, readable UI focused on strategic clarity
  • PC-first design with competitive and single-player modes

Development History

Developed by Taka Studio, the project evolved through rapid prototyping into a full-scale strategy game, focusing on systemic depth, readability, and meaningful decisions every turn.
